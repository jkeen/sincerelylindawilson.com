The Character, Linda Wilson

Linda Wilson is a good hearted 44 year old, who lives with her husband and two children somewhere in the midwestern United States. She often misunderstands a product’s intended use, takes suggestions from advertisements literally, and always lacks the common sense most of us take for granted.

If Linda Wilson was a real person, she would have died at an early age.
